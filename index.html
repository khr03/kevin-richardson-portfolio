About Kevin

Kevin Richardson, PhD, is a seasoned UX executive with over 30 years of experience at the intersection of cognitive psychology, user experience design, and product strategy. With a doctorate in Cognitive Psychology, Kevin brings a unique research-driven approach to simplifying complex systems. He has led UX teams across industries—from scientific instrumentation and enterprise software to advertising platforms and motorsports analytics.

Kevin's leadership spans consulting and in-house roles at companies like Siemens, Infragistics, INVIDI, and AT&T. He is known for applying deep user research to high-stakes environments, and for mentoring multidisciplinary teams through large-scale digital transformations. His design ethos blends clarity, precision, and empathy.
